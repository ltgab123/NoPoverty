Formation Of The Problem Statement

OUR PROCESS OF FINALISING THE PROBLEM STATEMENT


Firstly we all took interviews of some people and then took out the common as well as the different opinions of the people.


Then we all made an individual problem statement and after a little bit of discussion we concluded the ideas and came to the final problem statement.



Problem Statement


Although “No Poverty” is a very popular topic. Still people do not know about it. As a result, people do not know the steps taken by the government. Moreover, we want to change people’s mindset about poverty and make sure that they do not take it lightly.
