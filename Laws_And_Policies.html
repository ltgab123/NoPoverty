Laws And Policies

Law 1

Labour Laws :-

Labour laws may include provisions for minimum wage, working conditions, and protection against discrimination, which can be particularly important for those in low-income jobs.

Law 2

Access to Legal Representation :-

In many jurisdictions, individuals have the right to legal representation, and there are often legal aid services available for those who cannot afford a lawyer.

Housing Rights

Laws also exist to protect individuals from unfair eviction, ensure habitable living conditions, and provide access to affordable housing.

Education

Laws also guarantee access to education for all children, regardless of their economic status. This can include provisions for free or subsidized schooling.

Policy 1

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana :-

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana is a social welfare flagship programme of the Indian Government which was launched in the year 2015. The objective of this scheme is for providing housing at an affordable price to the weaker sections of the society, urban poor, rural poor and lower-income group people.

Policy 2

Antyodaya Anna Yojana :-

Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) is one of the biggest initiatives undertaken by the Government of India. It is a public distribution system scheme that was implemented in India in 2000. The main purpose of the system is to provide food security to the people and to end hunger in India.
